About HSD

It's 2000. People across the country are faced with an excessive serving of media and messages for daily consumption. There's intense competition formindspace, and victory belongs to those who provide the most engaging content and deliver it in the most compelling manner. That's where we come in...

Half Stop Down is a film production company of Bangladesh working with a strong commitment to global cinema. The company thrives on the art of storytelling using powerful visuals and rich cinematic language. One of the most talented and renowned advertisement film-maker of Bangladesh, Amitabh Reza is the mastermind behind this admirable venture. The advertisements he has made under the Half Stop Down label have garnered accolades from critics and audiences alike. These successes have helped Half Stop Down to earn a favorable reputation among its business clients as well.

But Half Stop Down is not resting on its laurels. We are constantly on the search for new talent. And this has led us to add a new director to our roster: AshfaqUzzaman Bipul a person of vision and craft. And, very valuably, of proven mettle.
